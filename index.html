Simple by design.

Three steps. No learning curve.

Step 1 — Set up once

Enter your name and add up to 5 emergency contacts. Grant location and SMS permissions. Takes under 2 minutes. Done forever.

Step 2 — Tap SOS when you need help

Press the big red button on the home screen. A 3-second countdown starts — giving you time to cancel if it's accidental.

Step 3 — Your people are notified

Your emergency contacts receive an SMS with your exact GPS location, a Google Maps link, and the time of the alert. No app needed on their end — just a regular text message.

Built for real emergencies.

Every decision was made with one question in mind: does this help someone in danger?

Works Without Internet

SMS alerts use your phone's cellular network, not data. Works in areas with poor internet but active signal. Even works in Airplane Mode off scenarios.

Real GPS Location

Fetches your live GPS coordinates the moment you tap SOS. If GPS takes too long, your last known location is used instantly. Every alert includes a one-tap Google Maps link.

3-Second Cancellation Window

Accidental taps happen. A countdown gives you time to cancel before anyone is notified. Confirmed alerts send immediately after.

Up to 5 Emergency Contacts

Add family, friends, or anyone you trust. Pick from your phonebook or add manually. All contacts receive the alert simultaneously.

Alert History

Every alert is saved locally with timestamp, location, and delivery status. Useful for reference — and for authorities if ever needed.

Your Data Stays on Your Device

No account. No cloud. No servers. Your contacts and location data never leave your phone.
